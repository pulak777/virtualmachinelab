Step 2: Click "Check" button.
  • if connection is correct, an alert appers. Click ok. Now, go to step 4.
  • if connection is wrong, an alert appers. Click ok. Now, go to step 3.
    • Step 3: Check the connection and either deattach the incorrect wire connection or Click on reset button and start from step 1.
    Step 4: Switch on the MCB.
    Step 5: Click anywhere on the autotransformer.
    Step 6: Slide the knob of the Rheostat to change the resistance.
    Step 7: Take readings from Rotor, Voltmeter, Ammeter and Watt-meter.
    Step 8: Click "Add to table" button to insert the values in the table.
    Step 9: Follow the procedure from step 6 to step 8, till you have the 6 readings on the table.
    Step 10: After taking minimum 6 readings, click the "Graph" button.
    Step 11: Click "Print" button, to print the full connections with graph and table.
